Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

1. Overview

Welcome to TinyImg ("the Site"). We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

2. Image Processing

All image compression, image-to-PDF conversion, and PDF merging happen entirely in your browser. We do not upload your files to any server. We do not store, retain, or use your images or documents in any way. All file data exists only within your browser session and is cleared when you close the page.

3. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Site, we may automatically collect the following non-personally identifiable information through third-party analytics (such as Google Analytics):

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system type
  • Date and time of visit
  • Referring page (referrer)
  • Anonymized IP address

4. Cookies

The Site uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies — required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies — help us understand site usage (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Advertising Cookies — third-party ad services (e.g., Google AdSense) may use cookies to display relevant ads

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site features.

Cookie Consent. When you first visit the Site, a cookie consent banner will appear at the bottom of the page. You may choose to "Accept" or "Reject" non-essential cookies (analytics and advertising). Your choice is stored locally in your browser for 365 days, after which you will be asked again. Essential cookies required for basic site functionality are always active and do not require consent.

If you previously accepted cookies and wish to change your preference, you can clear your browser's local storage for this site, and the consent banner will reappear on your next visit.

5. Third-Party Services

The Site may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — displays advertisements, may use cookies and web beacons
  • Google Analytics — analyzes site traffic and user behavior

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Security

Since image processing is entirely client-side (in your browser), we do not store any user image data. The Site uses HTTPS encryption to secure your connection.

7. Children's Privacy

The Site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with a new date. Continued use of the Site constitutes your agreement to the updated policy.
